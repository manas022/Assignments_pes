Location

My hometown Indore is in the Heart of India also called Madhya Pradesh. It is also located at a certain height as it was build on a the plateau among the mountains.

About

Industrial Capital

Indore has the largest economy in Madhya Pradesh and is the business and trading capital of the state. Pithampur Industrial Area, near Indore houses more than 1200 industrial units.

Food capital

Indore is the Food Capital of central India where Sarafa Bazaar being its heart. If one happens to be in Indore, he must surely visit this place which starts at 9 in the evening with various varities of shops begin to start to put up their stalls and you can enjoy such delicious, mouth watering dishes up until the late midnight. To put with up with some recommendations I would say Joshiji's Dahi Bada is must to start up with.

Education Capital

Indore, one of the prime cities of Madhya Pradesh is the center of education and learning. This place abounding in historical richness is home to many schools, colleges and universities.
The primary board in Indore is CBSE and there are a lot of school that follow this board however there are schools that also affialates with ICSE and NIOS boards as well.
Indore is the only city with both IIM and IIT lying in the same land space.
